$$
    \mathbf{std}(x) = \frac{\left\Vert x - \mathbf{mean}(x)\right\Vert}{\sqrt{n}}
$$</p>
<p>the function std in julia is defined as
$$
    \mathbf{std}(x) = \frac{\left\Vert x - \mathbf{mean}(x)\right\Vert}{\sqrt{n-1}}
$$</p>
